Venomous Snake

A marginalized individual navigating a city built to exclude him.

A young male snake with an unusually slender body and a pair of extremely elongated fangs. Despite lifelong discrimination, he remains sincere, energetic, and determined to belong.

Fox

A beneficiary of the system, uncertain of his place within it.

Born into a powerful family responsible for maintaining the existing order, the fox presents charm and confidence while concealing deep internal conflict.
