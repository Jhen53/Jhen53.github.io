Venomous Snake

A marginalized individual navigating a city built to exclude him.

A young male snake with a slender body and an unusually prominent pair of elongated fangs.

Despite lifelong systemic discrimination, he remains optimistic, hardworking, and sincere, driven by a desire to belong.

Fox

A beneficiary of the system, uncertain of his place within it.

Born into a powerful family within the existing social structure, the fox presents charm and confidence while concealing internal conflict and detachment.
